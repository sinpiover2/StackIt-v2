Quick rules & challenges (optional)
  • Drag only the top disc. You can’t place a bigger disc on a smaller disc.
  • Goal: move the whole stack to one pole (middle or right).
  • Try with 3–8 discs. Track your Moves and see how efficient you can be.
  • How many moves did it take for each disc count?
  • What patterns do you notice?
  • What do you think the minimum number of moves is for a given number of discs?
